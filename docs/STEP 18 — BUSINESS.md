# STEP 18 — BUSINESS.md

## Startup Business Strategy
Our objective is to maximize customer acquisition speed, maintain high margins, and ensure company survival. We target short-term rental (STR) hosts (Airbnb, VRBO) and multi-family residential landlords as our initial focus.

## Commercial Pricing Model

### 1. Hardware Purchase Fee
* **Retail Price:** **€129.00** per sensor device.
* **Unit BOM Cost:** **€18.00**.
* **Gross Hardware Margin:** **~86%**.
* This up-front hardware fee immediately covers our inventory capital, allowing us to build the next batch of sensors using the cash flow of our sales.

### 2. Software Subscription (SaaS)
* **Price:** **€8.00 / month** per active sensor (billed annually at €96.00).
* **AWS Serverless Cost per Device:** **< €0.05 / month** (MQTT message ingestion, DynamoDB storage, Cognito users).
* **Gross SaaS Margin:** **> 99%**.
* Focuses on generating recurring monthly revenue (MRR) to cover serverless cloud hosting and customer acquisition costs.

## Channel Sales Plan (First 5 Customers)
* Target regional property managers managing 10 to 50 properties rather than individual hosts. This allows us to secure our goal of 5 paying customers with just 1 or 2 contracts.
* Provide a **30-day money-back guarantee** to reduce purchase friction.
* Deliver localized hardware: pre-packaged boxes that hosts can plug directly into their wall outlet, requiring less than 5 minutes of Wi-Fi setup.
